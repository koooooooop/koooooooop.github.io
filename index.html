I'm Zhipeng Zhou, born on October 18, 2004, and currently a student in the X Innovation Experimental Class at Chongqing University. I have a strong interest in software development and technological innovation, with a current focus on research in artificial intelligence and deep learning.

My research interests are diverse, encompassing engineering principles, artificial intelligence, software design, linear algebra, and more.
