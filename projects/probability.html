Course Overview

This comprehensive course covers the fundamental concepts of probability theory and statistical analysis, providing a solid foundation for understanding random phenomena and data-driven decision making.

Key Topics

Probability Theory

Covers sample spaces, events, probability axioms, conditional probability, independence, random variables, and probability distributions.

Statistical Inference

Explores sampling distributions, point estimation, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, and Bayesian inference methods.

Random Variables

Examines discrete and continuous random variables, expectation, variance, moment generating functions, and common distributions.

Practical Applications

Applies probability and statistics concepts to real-world problems in engineering, science, economics, and data analysis.
